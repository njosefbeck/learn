Who We Are

BlueCurrent Hong Kong is a leading integrated PR and communications agency based in the heart of Hong Kong and we're proud to be a specialty brand of FleishmanHillard.

With the vitality of a boutique, backed by the resources of an international network, we deliver strategic communications programmes for agile, entrepreneurial companies across corporate, tech and consumer.

Results-driven and client-centric, we're a high performing team of consultants who live and breathe our client's values every day.

Our Expertise

  • External & Internal

    We have a rich experience in helping brands reach and engage with external audiences as well as managing award-winning employer branding and employee engagement programs.

  • Digital & Social

    We manage online marketing campaigns for Fortune 500 companies, who want to retain and attract talent, and we run social media campaigns for small startups who are looking to be heard.

  • Design & Branding

    From creating logos and designing infographics, to coming up with entire corporate identities, no job is too big or too small for our Creative Studio.

  • Media & Influencer

    We work daily with both the media and with influencers to make sure your messages get heard by the people who matter.

  • Video & Web

    Whether you need a new corporate video or a new responsive website we have in-house video production and web development teams on standby.
